Allowed vs Risky

The safest model is marketing services for licensed agents, not paid introductions into property transactions.

Good Marketing Agency Work

  • Build websites, landing pages, ads, photos, videos, SEO pages, and social posts.
  • Collect general enquiries and pass them to the licensed agent client.
  • Charge agents fixed marketing retainers, production fees, or ad-management fees.
  • Display the licensed agent's name, licence number, company, and contact path.

Needs Care

  • Charging per closed deal, per transaction, or per introduced tenant/buyer may look like estate agency remuneration.
  • Using listing photos, prices, or floor plans without written permission creates IP and accuracy risk.
  • Answering detailed buyer/tenant questions can drift into property advice.
  • Ranking agents or properties can create disclosure and misrepresentation issues.

Do Not Do Without Licence

  • Introduce a buyer, tenant, seller, or landlord to a client as a paid business.
  • Arrange viewings, negotiate price/rent, or handle offers.
  • Say you are the agent, broker, or representative for a property transaction.
  • Prepare or arrange signing of tenancy or sale documents.

Setup Checklist

Use this before taking money from agents or agencies.

Business Basics

  • Choose sole proprietor, partnership, or limited company.
  • Apply for Hong Kong business registration within one month of commencing business if operating in HK.
  • Open a separate business bank/payment account.
  • Prepare service agreement, invoice template, privacy notice, and cancellation terms.

Compliance Basics

  • Work only for verified licensed agents or agencies.
  • Put the licensed agent's details on property marketing pages.
  • Get written permission for every listing, image, video, and floor plan.
  • Use fixed marketing/service fees instead of transaction commission unless a lawyer confirms the structure.

Client Pitch

Simple positioning for outreach to licensed agents.

I help licensed Hong Kong property agents turn listings and district expertise into cleaner landing pages, short-form content, and trackable enquiry funnels. You remain the licensed agent handling agency work, client advice, viewings, negotiations, and transaction documents. I handle the marketing system and reporting.
