Emotional bottlenecks

Sometimes the slowest part of a job hunt is the story running underneath it.

Many talented people are not blocked by effort alone. They are blocked by protective beliefs built from rejection, scarcity, disappointment, or a workplace that taught them to stay small. These pages speak to those moments directly, then connect them to a practical next step.

What these beliefs often sound like

  • "I'm not good enough yet."
  • "I should just be grateful for what I have."
  • "I can't go through that kind of hurt again."
  • "That toxic workplace made me smaller than I am."
