For Malaysian waste generators

Not sure if the waste is ready for pickup? Start with a quick check.

If your scheduled waste store has contaminated containers, rags, filters, e-waste, or mercury-containing waste, send the basic details first. The team can then review whether the code, quantity, packaging, site access, and documentation are ready for the next step.

Quote Check Needs

  • Company name and person in charge
  • Site location and preferred pickup timing
  • Waste code or clear material description
  • Estimated quantity in kg, tonnes, drums, bags, pallets, or units
  • Storage age, label status, packaging condition, and photos
  • Any eSWIS, inventory, audit, or site access requirements

FAQ

          
Questions before you request a quote.

        

        

          

            What if I do not know the SW code?
            
Send photos, a material description, the container label, and where it was generated. The code still needs to be checked before quotation or pickup confirmation.

          

          

            Can I send photos first?
            
Yes. Photos of the waste, label, packaging and storage area help the team understand the case before advising next steps.

          

          

            What information is needed before quote?
            
Company, contact person, site location, waste code, quantity, packaging condition, storage age, photos, pickup urgency and any eSWIS or audit requirements.

          

          

            Does filling the form confirm pickup?
            
No. The form prepares your request. Pickup depends on code, quantity, packaging, licence fit, vehicle, receiver, documentation and site access.
