IC Educate privacy policy

Last updated: July 12, 2026

IC Educate helps parents, students, and teachers connect for tutoring. This policy explains what data the app collects, why it is used, and how users can delete their account and synced records.

What we collect

  • Account details such as name, email address, timezone, and chosen account type.
  • Tutor request details such as subject, level, area, lesson mode, timing, WhatsApp number, and notes.
  • Teacher profile details such as teaching subjects, syllabus, area, lesson availability, fee, and profile photo if provided.
  • Saved tutors, in-app marketplace messages, and worksheet or study progress linked to a signed-in account.
  • Approximate area suggestions only when the user taps a location button or chooses to share location.

What stays on the device

  • Some demo activity and previews are stored in browser or app local storage for faster use on the same device.
  • StudioPrime folder browsing in the app reads local files on the device and does not upload that folder by default.
  • Teacher promo media remains device-local unless separate production storage is added later.

How we use data

  • To match students with suitable tutors and let IC Educate follow up on valid requests.
  • To publish teacher profiles inside the tutor directory when the teacher has chosen public listing.
  • To keep saved tutors, messages, and learning preferences in sync across signed-in devices.
  • To operate worksheets, study resources, and marketplace support features.

What we do not do

  • We do not sell personal data.
  • We do not run advertising SDKs inside the current Android wrapper app.
  • We do not access location in the background.
  • We do not upload raw GPS coordinates; location is only used to suggest a nearby area label.

Account deletion and retention

Signed-in users can delete their account from the app profile flow or from the dedicated account deletion page. Deleting an account removes the auth user and linked synced records used by the app, including synced student profiles, tutor requests tied to that account, saved tutors, synced marketplace messages, and linked teacher profiles created while signed in.

If a teacher profile or request was submitted before signing in and is not linked to an account, contact support to remove it manually.
