Best channel mix right now

Build tutor supply first, then add student demand on top of a working WhatsApp close.

1. Get tutor contacts now

Start with teachers before students. Aim for 30 to 50 tutor leads in one market and a tight subject set so you can actually match the first requests fast.

2. Organic Facebook first

Post in tutor groups, parent groups, and area groups from a trusted Page, then answer comments and DMs manually. This is the cheapest way to test which offer gets replies.

3. Paid next: Click to WhatsApp

Once replies are converting, run Meta ads that open WhatsApp chats. Use both Facebook and Instagram placements, not Instagram-only brand ads.
